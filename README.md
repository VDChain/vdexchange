## Overview

VDExchange is the official centralized cryptocurrency exchange (CEX) being developed as part of the VDChain ecosystem. Designed to provide a professional, high-performance trading experience, VDExchange will offer spot trading, advanced order types, and deep liquidity for VDC and major cryptocurrency pairs.

VDExchange bridges the gap between the decentralized VDChain ecosystem and traditional crypto trading — giving users a familiar, feature-rich exchange experience while keeping VDC at the center of the trading economy.

---

## Planned Features

### Trading
- Spot trading for VDC and major pairs
- Advanced order types — Market, Limit, Stop-Loss, Take-Profit
- Real-time order book and trade history
- Professional trading charts (TradingView integration)
- Mobile-friendly trading interface

### Asset Support
- VDC / USDT trading pair (native VDChain)
- Major pairs — BTC, ETH, BNB, and more
- Fast deposits and withdrawals
- Multi-chain support

### Security & Compliance
- KYC / identity verification
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Cold wallet asset storage
- Advanced fraud detection

### User Experience
- Clean, intuitive trading interface
- Real-time price alerts
- Portfolio overview and P&L tracking
- Referral and reward programs
